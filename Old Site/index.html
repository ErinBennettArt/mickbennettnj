Greetings!

"Welcome to my website," said the writer to—whom?

Who reads/checks out these things? I admit I often don't. That may have more to do with internet ignorance than curiosity concerning other's views on the written word. I'm always up for that. Here's where you'll find my novels, notification of new publications, personal notes, and links. And of course the blog part of the website. If you're dreading political name calling, contemplating a nationalistic chest thump, or fretting the insensitive remark, fear not. Oh, I won't promise they'll be no essays or opinion pieces, but generally, if you're at all interested in writing, language, or communication of all kinds between people of all kinds, you may find something you'll want to read in the blog. I'm a big fan of the written word.
